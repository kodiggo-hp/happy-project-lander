Who Is Faye Torres?

She's a wife. A mom of two. A former advertising professional who immigrated from the Philippines to Canada.

After years of sending money home (batang bayan), sacrificing time, and wondering "is this all there is?" — Faye discovered a way to create income without trading her most precious resource: time with family.
